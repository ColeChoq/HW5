SCRABBLE

Welcome to scrabble. To start the game click the "Set Rack" button. This will give you your first set of tiles. After that you can drag each tile to a sqare to create a word. When you're ready to submit a word click the "Submit Word" button. If you would like to replace one tile just click the "x" on the tile. If you would like to replace your whole rack click the "Reset Rack" button. You can restart your game at any time with the "Restart Game" button. Enjoy!

Remeber, all tiles must be placed adjacent to at least one other tile
